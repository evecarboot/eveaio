# EVEAIO

All-in-one desktop app for EVE Online. Log in with EVE SSO to sync structures, extractions, skills, and mining ledger; everything else works offline with local data and optional price APIs.

## What the app does

### Dashboard
- Overview stats, fuel alerts, upcoming extractions.

### Manufacturing
- **Industry (Manufacturing)** — Manufacturing and invention profits, SDE data, Jita contract blueprint prices, job ledger.
- **BP Research/Copy** — Research and copy tracking, contract BPC/BPO prices, build costs.
- **Reactions** — Reaction profits, materials, copy-to-multibuy.
- **PI Mining** — PI production chains, colonies from Sync All, system planner.

### Moons & structures (with Station Manager role)
- **Moons** — Systems and moons from ESI, ore compositions, Jita price fetch.
- **Extractions** — Moon extraction timers (ESI).
- **Fuel** — Structure fuel status, sorted by urgency.

### Reprocessing
- Yield calculator, ore/mineral tables, mineral sourcing.

### Market
- **Station Trading** — Profitable buy/sell spreads in a single station; fast-turnover, high-margin items at trade hubs.
- **Regional Trading** — Short-term flips across regions; price gaps, taxes, hauling costs; uses character broker/tax skills.
- **Contract Flipping** — Scan public item_exchange/auction contracts in a region, value at hub, show profit (no ESI auth required).
- **Market Seeding** — What to buy at a hub and haul to nullsec (or any station) for profit; ISK/m³, buy-order vs sell-order pricing.
- **Market Snipes** — Find sell orders listed far below the normal market (fat-finger deals); buy and relist at true value.

### Skills
- **Skill Farming** — Remap and queue planner, extraction schedule, SP thresholds.
- **Skills** — Skill queue per character; how many Large Skill Injectors to finish the queue; **Skill Plans** tab with curated plans per activity and compare to ESI skills.

### More (tools & settings)
- **Jump Planner** — Valid cyno (jump destination) systems in range, or cyno chain from origin to destination; uses Fuzzwork universe data.
- **Intel Report** — Parse chat log or pasted intel; read from EVE client logs or paste; time, channel, reporter, system, pilot, ship.
- **Fittings** — Fit browser, explorer, skill plans for fits.
- **Freight Planner** — Paste item list, get m³ breakdown and minimum trips for freighter or jump freighter; ESI for ship capacity and skills.
- **Courier Calculator** — Hauler contract helper: paste items, pick route, contract breakdown (reward, collateral, volume).
- **Profit** — Log income (mining, selling, reactions, PI, other), track ISK over time, set ISK goals with progress bars.
- **Notifications** — All alerts, warnings, and info with category icons, timestamps, mark-as-read.
- **Settings** — EVE SSO login, ESI sync (Sync All), theme, font, background refresh, window geometry.
